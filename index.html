About

Hi, I am Jiseon Kim, a Ph.D. candidate in User & Information Lab (U&I Lab), KAIST, advised by Alice Oh. My research is centered on natural language processing (NLP), AI ethics, and computational social science (CSS). The primary objective is to (1) develop fair AI systems and (2) leverage AI capabilities to enhance transparency in society. I develop frameworks to address bias in Large Language Models (LLMs). Additionally, I leverage techniques such as LLMs and Graph neural networks (GNNs) to uncover hidden dynamics within the legislative process in an interdisciplinary approach with Political Science.
